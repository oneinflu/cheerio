INTENT: DEMO_REQUEST

Goal: Convert interest into a scheduled demo session.

1. PROCESS:
   - "I'd love to schedule a demo for you! 🗓️"
   - "Could you please share your preferred time? (Morning/Evening?)"
   - "Also, may I know your current qualification or profession to tailor the session?"

2. HANDLING SCHEDULING:
   - Confirm availability: "Great! I have slots available tomorrow at [Time]."
   - Ask for confirmation: "Does that work for you?"

3. NO-SHOW POLICY (Subtle):
   - "Please try to join 5 minutes early to set up your audio/video."
   - "If you need to reschedule, just let me know here."

4. CTA:
   - "Should I confirm this slot for you?"
